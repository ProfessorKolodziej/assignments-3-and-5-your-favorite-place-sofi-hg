Richmond, VA

Richmond, Virginia is the capital of Virginia full of delicious restaurants, vital United States history, and local art. Built next to the James River, the city has wonderful water views and beautiful local flora and fauna. Established in 1775, it is one of America's oldest cities and a witness to both the Revolutionary and Civil War.

Travel Tips

  • The public transit is free for use and takes you anywhere around richmond
  • Always look out for music festivals as there is almost always one happening
  • Take advantage of local farmers markets with fresh farm to table ingredients and products
  • Bring allergy medication if you are allergic to pollen as there are many trees and plants that will have you sneezing in spring
  • Make sure if you swim in the James River that the water is flowing, still water can have harmful bacteria
